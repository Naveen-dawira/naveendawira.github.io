More About IoBM

Sports

IoBM encourages an active lifestyle through a wide range of sports activities. Students participate in cricket, football, basketball, and indoor games, promoting teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness.

ECA's

Extra-curricular activities at IoBM help students explore their talents beyond academics. These activities include debates, dramatics, competitions, and workshops that build confidence and leadership skills.

Societies

Student societies at IoBM provide platforms for intellectual growth and collaboration. Academic, cultural, and social societies allow students to engage in events, discussions, and community initiatives.
